SSL Initiative

As a company, Pattr believes in providing our users with the highest possible security, which is why our team is hard at work building upon and improving Pattr's security, including efforts such as message encryption and password protection.

As part of this process, we have decided to upgrade our security, by intending on purchasing an SSL Certificate. SSL is a technology that allows data traveling between its users and the server to be transmitted securely and encrypted.

While we don't store messages on our servers, we do pass the messages through, scanning for certain commands such as '/nick', or '/users'. This does not store any messages on our servers, and we intend to keep it that way in effort to promote the highest security and enjoyable user experience and trust.

SSL Certificates are not cheap, and we need your help to purchase one. If every user donates $1, we will have enough money to buy the certificate, enabling HTTPS on our website and allowing for a more secure transaction between Pattr and its users.
